# Historical Strategy Intelligence

> **Disclaimer**: This module is strictly for educational, academic, and strategic research purposes.

The Strategy Nexus analyzes historical conflicts to predict outcome probabilities for modern high-resilience scenarios.

## ⚔️ Domain Scope

DISHA contains a high-fidelity dataset of **32+ major historical conflicts**, analyzed by:
- **Strategy Type**: (Guerrilla, Conventional, Attrition, Deception, etc.)
- **Outcome Variables**: Terrain, Technology Gap, Morale, and Supply Line integrity.
- **Era Mapping**: Ancient, Medieval, Early Modern, Modern, and Contemporary.

---

## 📉 Simulation Engine

The `HistoricalSimulationEngine` provides probabilistic outcome analysis based on a multi-factor effectiveness matrix.

| Parameter | Impact |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Terrain Factor** | Up to 3.0x multiplier for defender in mountain terrain. |
| **Technology Gap** | Logarithmic scaling — diminishing returns on tech advantage. |
| **Morale** | Critical threshold; low morale can void a 2x force advantage. |

## 🎨 Discovery Dashboard

The `/disha/apps/web` dashboard includes an interactive Strategy Map:
- **Timeline View**: Vertical chronological explorer.
- **Heatmaps**: Visualizing conflict density across geographic regions.
- **Scenario Simulator**: Input custom force ratios and strategies to see Predicted vs. Historical outcomes.
