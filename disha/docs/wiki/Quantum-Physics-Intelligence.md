# Quantum Physics Intelligence

DISHA Layer 6 provides a unified engine for high-fidelity physics simulations and space intelligence.

## ⚛ engines

### 1. Quantum Simulator
A high-performance Qiskit-powered simulator for testing quantum gate circuits and entanglement scenarios.
- **Bell States**: Automated testing of quantum correlations.
- **GHZ States**: Multi-qubit entanglement simulation.

### 2. Molecular Dynamics (Project SETU)
PyTorch-accelerated engine for simulating structural stress and material resilience. This is used by the **Resilience Forecaster** to predict infrastructure failure thresholds during natural disasters.

---

## 🌌 Space Intelligence

DISHA integrates real-time feeds for celestial monitoring:
- **NEO Feed**: Tracking Near-Earth Objects and their impact trajectories.
- **Orbital Simulator**: Calculating Keplerian orbits for satellite resilience.
- **NASA APOD**: Automated ingestion of high-resolution celestial imagery for visual training.

---

## 🛡️ Unified Field Theory

DISHA maintains a catalog of both **Classical** and **Suppressed Physics** theories. It uses a relevance scorer to match modern resilience challenges against historical (and sometimes fringe) physics concepts to discover novel energy-management solutions.
