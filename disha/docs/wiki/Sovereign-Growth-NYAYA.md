# Sovereign Growth & Project NYAYA

DISHA v6 is designed not just for defense, but for **Intelligent Governance** and national policy resilience.

## ⚖️ Project NYAYA (Judicial Intelligence)

Project NYAYA is an automated regulatory auditing engine.
- **Policy Mapping**: Correlating autonomous system decisions against legal and regulatory frameworks.
- **Confidence Gating**: Actions made by the AGI are screened for ethical and policy compliance before execution.
- **Immutable Audit**: All sovereign decisions are logged into a high-integrity, tamper-evident registry.

## 🌍 Global Intelligence Sync (GVM)

The **Global Volatility Multiplier (GVM)** is a proprietary index that correlates international market and geopolitical pulses with domestic priorities.

| Metric | Factor |
|:--- |:--- |
| **Market Pulse** | Currency volatility and supply chain friction. |
| **Geopolitical Pulse** | Regional instability and policy shifts. |
| **Domestic Resiliency** | Current national infrastructure health (SETU status). |

By correlating these, DISHA can predict "Resilience Funding Priority" and recommend proactive resource allocation for the nation.
