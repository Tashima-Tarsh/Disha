# Scientific Intelligence (SETU & VARUNA)

The scientific layer of DISHA bridges the gap between digital intelligence and physical reality.

## 🌊 Project VARUNA (Weather Intelligence)

Project VARUNA is a high-fidelity weather impact forecaster.
- **Kafka OSINT Streams**: Ingesting real-time global weather telemetry.
- **High-Resolution Impact**: Mapping storm trajectories and rainfall onto the national infrastructure digital twin.

---

## 🏗️ Project SETU (Infrastructure Resilience)

Project SETU (Structure) uses the Molecular Dynamics engine to predict failure points in critical physical assets.

### The Scientific Handshake
When Project VARUNA predicts a high-intensity weather event, Project SETU automatically initiates a **Stress-Test Simulation** for the affected geographic sector.

1.  **Ingestion**: VARUNA receives high-wind alerts.
2.  **Mapping**: The digital twin identifies bridges and power grids in the trajectory.
3.  **Simulation**: SETU runs physics-based stress simulations on these structures.
4.  **Recommendation**: DISHA alerts local maintainers with high-precision "Risk of Failure" scores.
