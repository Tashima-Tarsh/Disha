## [v6.0.0] — 2026-04-16
### 🛡️ The Monorepo Revolution (Sovereign Intelligence)
The v6 release represents a fundamental leap in architecture, moving from a multi-layer framework to a unified, high-scale monorepo designed for national-tier resilience.

#### 🏛️ Architecture & Governance
- **Monorepo Transition** — Restructured the entire ecosystem into decoupled clusters: `/apps` (UX), `/services` (Microservices), and `/ai` (Sovereign Core).
- **Sovereign Intelligence Core** — Upgraded the cognitive engine with the **National Awareness Layer** for real-time telemetry ingestion.
- **Unified Health Registry** — Implemented a centralized registry for monitoring inter-service dependencies.

#### ⚡ Intelligence & Reasoning
- **7-Stage Cognitive Loop v2** — Optimized the deliberation stage for consensus-gated sovereign decision making.
- **Project VARUNA x SETU** — Established deep-coupling between weather intelligence and infrastructure resilience simulators.
- **Project NYAYA** — Initial integration of the Judicial Intelligence module for high-confidence regulatory auditing.

#### 🔧 Security & Recovery
- **Autonomous Self-Healing** — Integrated a proactive layer within `disha_mythos` that auto-rolls back vulnerable service updates and restarts crashed containers.
- **Sentinel Hardening** — Full remediation of 30+ legacy vulnerabilities (lodas-es, axios) and stabilization of the Biome/Flake8 pipelines.
